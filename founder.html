Anshul Jakhar

Anshul Jakhar is the visionary behind the OceanCell™ Sustainable Project. With a strong drive to combat climate change and an innovative approach to green hydrogen production, he has dedicated his work to creating an affordable, accessible, and globally impactful solution for clean energy and water generation through seawater electrolysis.

His goal is not just to build a product, but a global cooperative movement that empowers cities, industries, and individuals to transition toward sustainable energy — one OceanCell at a time.
