About the Project

The OceanCell™ Hydrogen Generator System, founded by Anshul Jakhar, is a revolutionary solution to global energy and water crises. By using renewable energy to extract hydrogen, chlorine, oxygen, bleach, and water from seawater, OceanCell paves the way for affordable clean fuel, chemical supplies, and drinking water — while tackling climate change head-on.

Core Features

Green Hydrogen Fuel

Produce clean H2 for cars, homes, and industry powered by solar and wind.

Clean Water Recovery

Hydrogen combustion creates pure water — a sustainable solution for arid regions.

Chlorine & Bleach Production

Use NaOH and Cl2 to produce bleach for sanitation, public health, and industry.

Smart, Safe, Modular

Automatic safety shutdown, removable tanks, and maintenance-ready modular design.

Global Impact Potential

Designed for cooperative deployment in coastal cities and communities worldwide.

UN SDG Aligned

Directly supports 7 Sustainable Development Goals with measurable indicators.
